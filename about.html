कौन (who)

We are the first and only student organization at Purdue that gives South Asian students opportunity to learn public speaking skills in Hindi. That being said, we do not want to limit ourselves to students who know how to speak Hindi and we welcome students who are curious to know about and learn Hindi.

क्या (what)

The goal of this organization is to improve each student member's public speaking skills. In order to do that, we discuss and practice various techniques one needs to know to be a great public speaker. To recognize each member's public speaking progress over a semester, we organize a Speech/Debate competition, which is open to both members and non-members.

क्यों (why)

Purdue is home to a big number of Hindi speaking students and we believe there should be a platform for students who participated or wanted to participate in Hindi oriented events and competitions during their high school. Also, a significant percentage of the Hindi speaking population wants to go back to their homes to create a difference through entrepreneurship or leadership engagement in the society. Our aim is to help those Boilermakers become better speakers.
